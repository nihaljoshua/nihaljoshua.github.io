Max Temperature

This is a very interesting graph since unlike other graphs you see a larger cluster of data spread out of a specific region. There are a couple of data points above 100 F and a few data points below -50F. But the temperature bracket of 50 to 100F can be seen between -50 and 30 latitude. We can see a similar trend of temperature range (0 to 50F) in latitude 30 to 70. The remaining data points for the temperature range (0 to -50F) falls in the latitude range 45 to 75.
