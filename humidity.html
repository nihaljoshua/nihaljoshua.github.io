Humidity

The data on the percentage of humidity is mostly spread across -20 to 70 Latitude. One will notice an amount of clustered data for the above mentioned Latitude coordinates at the highest level of humidity of 100%. The percentage of humidity below 100% is spread widely across the same region. Since for humidity to at zero or close to zero there needs to be a high level of dryness which is not commonly found. But we do see a few data points of 0% humidity around 60 to 75 Latitude.
